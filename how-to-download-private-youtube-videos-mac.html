YouTube video privacy levels explained

YouTube has three privacy levels for videos, and each one affects whether you can download it:

Public videos

Visible to everyone, appear in search results and on the channel page. These are the easiest to download — just paste the URL. Star Video Downloader handles them out of the box.

Unlisted videos

Accessible to anyone with the direct link, but don't appear in search or on the channel page. Teachers, companies, and content creators often use unlisted videos for sharing content with specific audiences. Star Video Downloader downloads these the same way as public videos — just paste the URL.

Private videos

Only visible to specific Google accounts selected by the uploader. These cannot be downloaded by third-party tools, including Star Video Downloader. The video owner would need to change the privacy setting or share the video through other means.

How to download age-restricted videos

YouTube age-restricts videos that contain mature content — violence, explicit language, sensitive topics. Normally, you need to sign in and confirm your age to watch them. This makes downloading harder because most tools can't pass the age verification check.

Star Video Downloader handles age-restricted content automatically. The underlying yt-dlp engine has built-in methods to bypass age gates without requiring you to sign in. Just paste the URL like any other video.
