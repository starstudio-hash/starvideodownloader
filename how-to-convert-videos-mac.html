Common Conversion Scenarios

MOV to MP4

iPhone and Mac screen recordings save as MOV files, which aren't always compatible with Windows, Android, or web platforms. Converting MOV to MP4 ensures your videos play everywhere.

MKV to MP4

MKV files from downloads or rips don't play in QuickTime or many media players. Converting to MP4 makes them compatible with macOS preview, AirPlay, and sharing via Messages or email.

Video to MP3 (audio extraction)

Extract audio from music videos, podcasts, lectures, or interviews. Select MP3 as the output and the converter strips the video track, keeping just the audio.

WebM to MP4

WebM files downloaded from the web often don't play natively on macOS. Converting to MP4 gives you full QuickTime and Finder preview support.
