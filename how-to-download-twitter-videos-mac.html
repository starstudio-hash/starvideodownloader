Why saving Twitter/X videos on Mac is difficult

Twitter and X don't provide a native download button for videos in tweets. Right-clicking a video only gives you "Copy video address" which leads to an HLS stream — not a playable file. Browser extensions are unreliable and often break when Twitter updates its layout.

Star Video Downloader takes a different approach: paste any tweet URL and the app extracts the video directly from Twitter's servers in the highest available quality, saving it as a clean MP4 file on your Mac.

How to Download Twitter/X Videos on Mac — Step by Step

1

Download Star Video Downloader

Get the free app from starvideoapp.com. Unzip and move to Applications. On first launch, right-click and select Open to bypass Gatekeeper.

2

Find the tweet with the video

Open twitter.com or x.com in Safari or Chrome and navigate to the tweet containing the video you want to save.

3

Copy the tweet URL

Click the share icon on the tweet and select "Copy link", or copy the URL directly from your browser's address bar. It will look like https://x.com/username/status/...

4

Paste into Star Video Downloader

Open Star Video Downloader, click the URL field, and paste with ⌘V. The app automatically detects the video in the tweet.

5

Click Download

Hit Download and the video saves directly to your Mac as an MP4 file. Click the folder icon to reveal it in Finder.

Tip: Both twitter.com and x.com URLs work. You can also paste URLs from Twitter's mobile app — tap Share on any tweet and Copy Link.
