Why downloading Vimeo videos is different

Unlike most video platforms, Vimeo offers multiple privacy levels that affect how videos can be accessed and downloaded:

  • Public — visible to everyone and listed on Vimeo. Easiest to download.
  • Unlisted — not listed in search results, but anyone with the link can view it.
  • Password-protected — requires a password to watch. You need the password to download.
  • Embedded-only — can only be watched on specific websites where the creator embedded the player.
  • Vimeo Pro private — restricted to specific Vimeo users. May not be downloadable without proper access.
