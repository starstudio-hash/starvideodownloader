What are YouTube Shorts?

YouTube Shorts are vertical, short-form videos up to 60 seconds long. They're YouTube's answer to TikTok and Instagram Reels — designed for quick, mobile-first content like reactions, tutorials, memes, and highlights. Shorts are displayed in a dedicated feed with swipe-to-scroll navigation.

Despite their popularity, YouTube doesn't provide a download button for Shorts on desktop. The mobile app offers limited offline saving in some regions, but there's no native way to save a Short as a regular video file on your Mac.

Why YouTube Shorts are tricky to download

  • No desktop download button — YouTube only offers offline saving for Shorts in the mobile app, and only in select regions
  • Different URL format — Shorts use youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID instead of the standard youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID, which can confuse some download tools
  • Vertical aspect ratio — Shorts are filmed in 9:16 portrait mode; some tools crop or distort the video if they don't handle vertical content properly

Star Video Downloader handles all three issues. It recognizes both URL formats, preserves the vertical aspect ratio, and saves Shorts as standard video files.
