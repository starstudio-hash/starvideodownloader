Why downloading Dailymotion videos is tricky

Dailymotion doesn't offer a built-in download button for most videos. The platform uses adaptive streaming (HLS/DASH) that splits video into small segments, making it impossible to simply right-click and save. Browser extensions are unreliable and frequently break when Dailymotion updates their player.

Online Dailymotion downloaders exist, but they typically cap at 720p, show aggressive ads, and require uploading the URL to a third-party server. A desktop app like Star Video Downloader handles the streaming protocol locally, giving you the full quality without any of those limitations.

Dailymotion vs YouTube — downloading differences

While the download process is the same (paste URL, click download), there are a few differences worth noting:

  • Quality: Dailymotion tops out at 1080p for most content, while YouTube offers up to 8K
  • Formats: Dailymotion primarily uses MP4, making downloaded files universally compatible
  • Geo-restrictions: Some Dailymotion content is region-locked. Star Video Downloader downloads whatever is accessible from your location
  • Speed: Dailymotion downloads tend to be faster since file sizes are smaller
