Why Facebook videos are hard to save

Facebook doesn't make it easy to download videos from its platform:

  • No download button — most Facebook videos don't have a save-to-device option
  • Embedded player blocks right-click — the custom video player prevents standard browser "Save Video As" methods
  • Facebook Reels — short-form vertical videos use a different format and playback system, making them harder to capture
