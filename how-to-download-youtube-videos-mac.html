Step-by-Step Guide

Downloading YouTube Playlists on Mac

To download an entire YouTube playlist, copy the playlist URL (it contains playlist?list=) and paste it into Star Video Downloader. With a Pro license, all videos in the playlist are queued automatically. You can deselect individual videos before starting.

Downloading YouTube Audio as MP3 on Mac

If you only need the audio from a YouTube video — for example, a music video, podcast, or lecture — use the built-in conversion tab. Download the video first, then use the Convert tab to extract audio as MP3, AAC, FLAC, or WAV. No quality loss compared to online converters.

Downloading YouTube Videos with Subtitles

Star Video Downloader Pro can embed subtitles directly into downloaded videos. In the download settings, enable "Embed Subtitles" and select your preferred language. The subtitles will be permanently embedded in the output file.
