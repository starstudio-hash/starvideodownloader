Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. The free version lets you download up to 5 videos per day in up to 1080p. No trial period, no expiration — free forever.

What sites are supported?

Over 1000 sites including YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter/X, Reddit, Instagram, TikTok, Twitch, Dailymotion, and many more.

What macOS version do I need?

Star Video Downloader requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. It runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

Is the Pro license a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment of $5. Pay once, use forever. No recurring charges.

Can I use Pro on multiple Macs?

Yes. Each Pro license can be activated on up to 3 Macs at the same time.

How do I get my license key?

After purchasing, you'll receive your license key via email instantly. Just paste it into the app's Settings to unlock Pro.

Is this a good 4K Video Downloader alternative?

Yes. Star Video Downloader supports the same sites, offers 8K quality, and costs just $5 one-time — cheaper than most alternatives with no subscription. See the full comparison →

Does it work on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4)?

Yes. Star Video Downloader runs natively on Apple Silicon Macs as well as Intel Macs.

Can I download YouTube playlists?

Yes, with a Pro license. Paste the playlist URL and Star Video Downloader will queue all videos automatically. You can also choose which items to skip.

Can I download audio only (MP3)?

Yes. Star Video Downloader can extract audio and save it as MP3, AAC, FLAC, or other audio formats using the built-in conversion tool.

Is it safe to use?

Yes. Star Video Downloader is a native macOS app distributed as a signed binary. It does not collect personal data, does not require an account, and all downloads happen directly on your Mac.

What video formats can I download in?

You can download in MP4, MKV, WebM, and more. The conversion tool also lets you convert between MP4, MKV, MOV, WebM, AVI, and extract audio as MP3, M4A, FLAC, WAV, and others.

Does it support SponsorBlock?

Yes. With a Pro license, Star Video Downloader integrates SponsorBlock to automatically skip or remove sponsored segments from downloaded YouTube videos.

What is the video repair tool?

The Pro video repair tool can fix corrupted, truncated, or unplayable video files using a multi-stage ffmpeg repair pipeline. It works entirely offline — no uploading files to any server. It handles missing moov atoms, broken indexes, corrupt frames, and audio desync issues.

How is this different from yt-dlp?

Star Video Downloader is a native macOS graphical interface built on top of yt-dlp and ffmpeg. You get all the power of yt-dlp without needing Terminal — just paste a URL and click download.

Can I download videos from Instagram or TikTok?

Yes. Star Video Downloader supports Instagram Reels, TikTok videos, Twitter/X videos, Reddit videos, and hundreds of other platforms — not just YouTube.
